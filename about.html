ABOUT US

Hi, I am Vismitha from VA Donation.
Our main aim is to deal with poverty and provide quality food, education and health-care to poor kids and orphans. We strive to grow more and more as we reach out to hold their hand. If you want to help these kids and provide them a brighter future, Join with us Today! Your single donation can change a life.

VISIT TO PARIVARIKEE

We as a team visited Parivarikee which is an all boys orphange loacted in West Bengal. We provided them with clothes and toys. We spent an entire day with them. It was a really great experience and we had lots of fun. Help us to conduct more such donation for these kids!

VISIT TO CHHAYA ORPHANAGE

Chhaya Orphange is a small orphange located in Kolkata. We visited there with our team and donated all essential needs.children were happy and enjoyed! help us do this more

VISIT TO EVERETT HOME

Everett Home is a Charity and Care Home for Children located in North Bengal. Our team visited there and taught the kids basic Maths, Science and English and also donated them books, pencils, pens notebooks etc. Help us provide quality education and share our knowledge with more such talented kids!
